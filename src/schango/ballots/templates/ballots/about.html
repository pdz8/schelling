Purpose

CrowdVerity is a crowd-powered method for importing facts from the real-world to the digital-world. Users of CrowdVerity need not trust a single central authority to tell them what is truth. They instead can query almost any fact from the the incentivized masses. Here are just a few scenarios that can benefit from CrowdVerity:

Insurance
Currently it is often the case that insurance companies determine themselves when conditions suffice to award payouts. This is a huge conflict of interest because they are the ones paying. It is no wonder why so many lawyers devote their careers to suing insurance companies.
Prediction
In any type of betting scenario there is the risk that a trusted authority could also be in on the bet. This immediately makes them untrustworthy.
Exchanges
The value of currencies ought to be set by whatever value the general public believes the currency to be worth. This is most easily accomplished decentralized.

Technologies

SchellingCoin

CrowdVerity uses the SchellingCoin mechanism to aggregate its inputs into truth. Read here to learn more about SchellingCoin's theory.

Ethereum

Ethereum is a Bitcoin 2.0 technology that serves as both a cryptocurrency and a platform for decentralized applications. Programs (contracts) running within Ethereum get all the same immutability and availability guarantees as transactions on the block-chain. CrowdVerity runs all of its ballots as contracts in the Ethereum platform. Read here to learn more about Ethereum.
